About

I am a final-year undergraduate at IIIT Hyderabad (CGPA 8.29/10, ML Major 9.14/10). My work is driven by a central observation: while current LLMs are fluent, they often struggle with verifiable reasoning under real-world constraints.

My research journey - spanning PayPal, Microsoft Research, and TCS Research - has moved from pure implementation to post-training optimization. I am currently focused on using Reinforcement Learning to align models for safety and privacy, and exploring how ideas from Learning Sciences can structure feedback to improve model reasoning. My goal is to build AI systems that are not just knowledgeable, but reliable enough for high-stakes deployment in healthcare and education.
